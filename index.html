Model United Nation 2k19

A full fledged event designed on the lines of the United Nations, Veltech university, Avadi is hosting its first edition of it's Model United Nations. A complete day of active debating and public speaking, the event is aimed to be pinnacle of communication and social events ever held in this University. Students from all over the university, including foreign students, are going to participate as delegates. Starting from General Assembly meeting to individual committee meets, delegates will be allotted their countries, committees and topics. ​ From hosting training workshops to the valedictory speech, the delegates would be adequately armed with the knowledge of the functioning of the United Nations. They will get equipped with the experience to participate in similar events outside the university.
